About PrintoKids PH

PrintoKids PH is a small business based in the Philippines that focuses on personalized printing services and customized souvenirs for different occasions and celebrations.

From customized merchandise to event giveaways, PrintoKids PH offers creative, affordable, and high-quality products that help clients make their events more meaningful and memorable.

Our Specialties:
  • Custom tote bags, mugs, stickers, labels, and tags
  • Personalized souvenirs and keychains
  • Interactive Pick & Press event booths
  • On-the-spot personalized giveaways
